Vision

This is a template for a simple marketing or informational website. It includes a large callout called the hero unit and three supporting pieces of content. Use it as a starting point to create something more unique.

Search

Search for glasses by prescription or by call number. Prescription searches can be broad or specific.

Add

Makes changes to the inventory here. This section will allow you to add and edit glasses that are currently in the inventory.
