Keyboard shortcuts

KeystrokeDescription
<tab> Move to the next input box
(Up arrow) Increment box by a small step (+0.5 for spherical, cylindrical and add. +5 for axis)
Shift + (Shift + Up arrow) Increment box by a large step (+1 for spherical, cylindrical and add. +10 for axis)
(Down arrow) Decrement box by a small step (-0.5 for spherical, cylindrical and add. -5 for axis)
Shift + (Shift + Down arrow) Decrement box by a large step (-1 for spherical, cylindrical and add. -10 for axis)
